Let's cook a delicious snack!

All you will need for this recipe is in the list bellow:

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 spoons of chocolate powder
  • 4 spoons of maize starch
  • 2 spoons of sugar

Mix all ingredients together and, will the help of an adult, put it into the microwave for 3 minutes. After that serve it in beautiful mugs and enjoy your hot chocolate.
